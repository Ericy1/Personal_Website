NASA (Summer 2021)

This summer I will be working with NASA to design and test software for an novel, optical terminal that will communicate with spacecraft from LEO to lunar orbit. I'll be implementing a Gimbal Monitor & Control system (GMC) with Python, RabbitMQ, and Google Protobuf to connect to both the Planewave telescope mount and the Monitor & Control Subsystem (MCS). GMC will also be updated to support Planewave acceptance testing and integration with the WFOV state machine. I'll also support testing and create automated pipelines with Docker and Ansible.
